100-Level Weekly Study Schedule

A balanced weekly plan to cover all your first-semester courses effectively. Adapt this template to your lecture schedule.

How to Use This Schedule

  • Be Consistent: The key is to study a little bit every day, not cram everything at once.
  • Active Recall: During "Practice" or "Review" sessions, don't just read. Use your cheat sheets to quiz yourself and solve problems without looking at the answers first.
  • 50/10 Rule: For each 1-hour block, study for 50 minutes and take a 10-minute break.
  • Be Flexible: This is a template. Adjust it based on your lecture times, assignment deadlines, and energy levels.
